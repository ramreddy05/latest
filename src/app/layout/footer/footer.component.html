Use this application to search prison location, probation/parole supervision information, current and historical addresses. The information you can access is based on your JNET security and application roles. Records are obtained and returned from the following sources:

AOPC
County Prison
County Probation
DOC
Domestic Relations
PA Dept of Labor & Industry
PBPP
PennDOT
PSP - CLEAN
WebCPIN

This application is not a replacement for the underlying applications. To perform additional searches, the underlying applications may be accessed via the JNET Portal homepage.
